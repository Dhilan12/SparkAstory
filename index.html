Our Mission

At Spark a Story, we believe every young voice deserves to be heard. Through hands-on workshops, storytelling circles, and real human connection, we guide kids in turning their ideas into powerful stories.

Workshops

Interactive sessions where kids brainstorm, write, and share stories together.

Community

A safe, supportive space where young writers encourage and inspire one another.

Publishing

Helping kids polish their work and showcase it to the world through books, zines, and readings.
