Our Mission

At Spark a Story, we believe every young voice deserves to be heard. Through free workshops, online resources, and community support, we help kids share their ideas and transform them into powerful stories.

Our next events/classes:
Writing and Publishing class: October 4th - October 26th, 10:00-11:00 every morning (Central Standard Time)
In this free class, students in grades 1 to 6 have the opportunity to learn valuable techniques and methods in creative writing, along with the skills needed to revise and critique manuscripts. All students will have the option to potentially self-publish their short stories in a collection through Spark a Story.
