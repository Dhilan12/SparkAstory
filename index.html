Our Mission

Spark a Story helps kids turn their ideas into powerful stories. Through workshops, storytelling circles, and mentorship, we guide young writers to grow their skills and confidence.

Workshops

Interactive sessions where kids brainstorm, write, and share stories together.

Community

A supportive space where young writers encourage and inspire one another.

Publishing

Helping kids polish their work and showcase it through books, zines, and readings.
