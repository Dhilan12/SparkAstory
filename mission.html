Our Mission

Spark a Story empowers the next generation of voices to bridge the gap between imagination and the page. Based in Frisco, Texas, our student-led movement provides the mentorship, workshops, and publishing platforms young writers need to turn a quiet passion into a global reach. We believe writing isn’t just an academic requirement—it’s a fundamental power every student should own.
