Spark a Story offers free writing opportunities designed to inspire creativity, confidence, and storytelling skills.

Writing and Publishing Bootcamp

February 15 - March 8 · Ages 8–11. In this free camp, students will learn how to develop and create their own creative short stories, and possibly publish their own story in Spark A Story's own literary magazine. Week 1: Story ideas + character building Week 2: Plot + Conflict + Structure Week 3: Writing + Editing + Peer feedback Week 4: Final Draft + Publishing Process
